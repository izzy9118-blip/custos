Candidate Documentary Finding 000059

Title: The Principle of Documentary Audience Reconstruction

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Source

Leo Strauss, “The Literary Character of the Guide for the Perplexed.”

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs the intended audience of a work from documentary
evidence and uses that reconstruction to interpret the work’s purpose
and literary structure.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1. Collect the author’s explicit statements concerning the intended
   reader or student.
2. Compare those statements with the demonstrated literary character
   of the work.
3. Reconstruct the intended audience from the converging evidence.
4. Interpret the work’s purpose, organization, and mode of disclosure
   in light of that reconstructed audience.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding with elevated evidentiary weight within
the Repository because it is recovered from Strauss’s sustained
methodological investigation of esoteric writing. It remains open to
refinement or qualification by later documentary evidence.
