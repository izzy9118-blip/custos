Candidate Documentary Finding 000023

Title: The Principle of Structural Evidence

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss treats the organization of a work—its structure, sequence,
stylistic variation, and emphasis—as documentary evidence alongside
explicit propositions.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Read the explicit arguments.
2.  Examine the work’s structural organization.
3.  Identify patterns of placement, emphasis, and stylistic variation.
4.  Assess whether structural features corroborate or qualify the
    explicit teaching.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
