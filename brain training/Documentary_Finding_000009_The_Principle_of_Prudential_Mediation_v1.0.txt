DOCUMENTARY FINDING 000009

Title: The Principle of Prudential Mediation

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss explains philosophical modes of communication by reconstructing
the prudential judgments required when philosophical inquiry exists
within political society.

Literary strategy is neither arbitrary nor merely stylistic. It is
presented as a rational response to competing obligations.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the enduring obligations governing the thinker.
2.  Identify tensions between those obligations.
3.  Reconstruct the range of reasonable responses available.
4.  Explain literary strategy as a prudential resolution of those
    tensions rather than as an isolated technique.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring confirmation elsewhere in
Persecution and the Art of Writing before promotion.
