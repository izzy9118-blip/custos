Candidate Documentary Finding 000019

Title: The Principle of Structural Priority

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss requires reconstruction of the overall plan, literary character,
and context of a work before assigning meaning to individual passages.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Reconstruct the work as a whole.
2.  Situate each passage within its broader context.
3.  Preserve the intelligibility of the transmitted text where possible.
4.  Resort to emendation only after contextual explanations have been
    exhausted.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
