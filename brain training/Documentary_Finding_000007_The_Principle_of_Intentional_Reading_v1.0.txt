# Documentary Finding 000007
## The Principle of Intentional Reading

Repository: Straussian Documentary Memory
Status: Documentary Finding (Provisional)

## Documentary Observation

Strauss increasingly treats understanding as the recovery of intentional communication rather than the extraction of isolated statements.

## Recovered Inquiry Operation

A philosophical work should be investigated as a deliberate act of communication, taking into account:

- Intended audience
- Historical circumstances
- Literary structure
- Rhetorical choices
- Explicit propositions

No single element should be interpreted in isolation from the others.
