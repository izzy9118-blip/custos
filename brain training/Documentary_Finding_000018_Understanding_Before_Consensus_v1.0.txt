Candidate Documentary Finding 000018

Title: The Principle of Understanding Before Consensus

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss evaluates an interpretive method by its capacity to understand
its subject rather than by its ability to generate scholarly consensus.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Evaluate the method according to its fitness for the subject.
2.  Do not reject a method solely because disagreement remains.
3.  Compare methods by explanatory adequacy.
4.  Recognize that difficult historical inquiry may legitimately produce
    disagreement.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
