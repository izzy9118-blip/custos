Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000110

The Principle of Documentary Functional Necessity

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes between literary devices that are merely present
and those that are functionally necessary for accomplishing the author’s
documentary purpose.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the literary device.
2.  Recover the author’s stated literary objective.
3.  Determine whether the objective depends upon that specific device.
4.  Distinguish essential from contingent literary techniques.

Documentary Observation
