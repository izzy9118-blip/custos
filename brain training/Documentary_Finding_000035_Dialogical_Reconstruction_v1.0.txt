Candidate Documentary Finding 000035

Title: The Principle of Dialogical Reconstruction

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs the literary form of the Guide as an approximation
of oral philosophical inquiry, treating explicit statements as openings
for investigation rather than final conclusions.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Determine the intended literary form.
2.  Adapt interpretation to that form.
3.  Treat explicit statements as initiating inquiry.
4.  Resolve questions through extended comparison and the work’s overall
    structure.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional pending corroboration elsewhere in Strauss’s corpus.
