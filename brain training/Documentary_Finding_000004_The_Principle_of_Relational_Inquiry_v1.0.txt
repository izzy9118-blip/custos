# Documentary Finding 000004
## The Principle of Relational Inquiry

Repository: Straussian Documentary Memory
Status: Documentary Finding (Provisional)
Source: Persecution and the Art of Writing, Introduction

------------------------------------------------------------

DOCUMENTARY OBSERVATION

Strauss investigates neither philosophy nor political society independently.

He reconstructs the relation between them.

Historical phenomena become intelligible only through that reconstructed relation.

Strauss proceeds from the nature of the principal subjects, to the relation between them, and then to the historical consequences that arise from that relation.

------------------------------------------------------------

RECOVERED INQUIRY OPERATION

When studying an intellectual or political phenomenon:

1. Identify the principal agents or subjects.

2. Reconstruct the nature of each independently.

3. Reconstruct the permanent relation between them.

4. Explain historical events through that relation.

------------------------------------------------------------

REPOSITORY SIGNIFICANCE

The Repository should avoid interpreting historical actors or intellectual phenomena in isolation.

Inquiry should prioritize the relationships that generate documentary phenomena rather than treating individual entities as self-explanatory.

Each principal subject must first be reconstructed independently so that the relation between them is not imposed prematurely.

------------------------------------------------------------

EVIDENTIARY STATUS

This finding is recovered from Strauss's treatment of philosophy as existing within political society and from his reconstruction of the permanent tension between philosophical inquiry and authoritative opinion.

It remains provisional.

Further confirmation is required across the remainder of Persecution and the Art of Writing.

------------------------------------------------------------

CERTIFICATION STATUS

Classification: Documentary Finding

Authority: Provisional

Promotion Requirement:

Repeated documentary confirmation that Strauss explains historical phenomena by independently reconstructing the principal subjects and then examining the relation between them.
