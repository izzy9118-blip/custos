Candidate Documentary Finding 000013

Title: The Principle of Exactness Before Esotericism

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss subjects reading between the lines to the prior requirement of
historical exactness.

Interpretation beyond the explicit text is legitimate only when it
satisfies a stricter standard of documentary evidence than a purely
literal reading.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Begin with explicit statements.
2.  Test whether the literal reading explains the evidence.
3.  Consider non-literal interpretation only if it increases historical
    exactness.
4.  Reject interpretations that reduce documentary exactness.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
