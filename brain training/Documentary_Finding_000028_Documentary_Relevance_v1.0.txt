Candidate Documentary Finding 000028

Title: The Principle of Documentary Relevance

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes ordinary textual features from documentary
phenomena that genuinely require historical explanation.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Record observable literary phenomena.
2.  Distinguish ordinary features from probative ones.
3.  Treat only genuinely explanatory phenomena as documentary evidence.
4.  Base interpretations on probative evidence.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
