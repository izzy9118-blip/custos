# Documentary Finding 000006
## The Principle of Investigating the Reader

Repository: Straussian Documentary Memory
Status: Documentary Finding (Provisional)

## Documentary Observation

Strauss does not limit inquiry to the historical author.

He also examines the assumptions brought by the modern interpreter.

Historical misunderstanding may originate as much from the reader's presuppositions as from deficiencies in the surviving documents.

## Recovered Inquiry Operation

1. Identify the assumptions governing the present reader.
2. Determine whether those assumptions are historically contingent.
3. Reconstruct the author's historical conditions before imposing contemporary expectations.
4. Allow the reconstructed conditions to reshape the reading.
