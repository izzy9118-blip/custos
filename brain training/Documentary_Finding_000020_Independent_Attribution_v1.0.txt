Candidate Documentary Finding 000020

Title: The Principle of Independent Attribution

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes the voice of the author from the voices of
characters and prohibits attributing a character’s views directly to the
author without independent justification.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the literary speaker.
2.  Distinguish the speaker from the historical author.
3.  Seek independent evidence for authorial attribution.
4.  Suspend attribution if adequate evidence is lacking.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
