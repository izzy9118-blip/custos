# Documentary Finding 000005
## The Principle of Form as Evidence

Repository: Straussian Documentary Memory
Status: Documentary Finding (Provisional)
Source: Persecution and the Art of Writing, Introduction (Reading Unit 5)

DOCUMENTARY OBSERVATION

Strauss does not treat literary form as merely ornamental.

He approaches the manner of writing as something that may itself require historical and philosophical explanation.

Writing is introduced only after reconstructing the permanent relation between philosophy and political society. Literary form is therefore investigated as a consequence of those conditions rather than as an isolated stylistic choice.

RECOVERED INQUIRY OPERATION

1. Study what is said.
2. Study how it is said.
3. Ask whether the literary form follows from the reconstructed conditions under which the work was written.
4. Avoid separating doctrine from presentation prematurely.

REPOSITORY SIGNIFICANCE

A documentary investigation cannot be confined to propositions alone.

Arrangement, structure, sequence, emphasis, omission, and presentation may themselves constitute documentary evidence.
