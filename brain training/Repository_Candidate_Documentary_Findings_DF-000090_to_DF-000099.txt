Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000090

The Principle of Explicit Methodological Adoption

Documentary Observation

Strauss explicitly adopts the interpretive procedure authorized by the
author and presents it as the proper method for the investigator.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover the author’s own methodological instructions.
2.  Determine the operations those instructions require.
3.  Explicitly adopt those operations as the method of inquiry.
4.  Conduct the reconstruction according to those adopted operations.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding. Strauss expressly states that ‘we may’
recover the secret teaching by connecting chapter with chapter and
secret word with secret word.
