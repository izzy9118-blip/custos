Candidate Documentary Finding 000063

Title: The Principle of Functional Documentary Hierarchy

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes works according to the different functions they
perform within an author’s intellectual project rather than by external
prestige or influence.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the distinct function assigned to each work.
2.  Reconstruct the author’s overall intellectual project.
3.  Determine how each work contributes to that project.
4.  Interpret relationships through functional roles rather than
    external measures.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional pending corroboration elsewhere in Strauss’s corpus.
