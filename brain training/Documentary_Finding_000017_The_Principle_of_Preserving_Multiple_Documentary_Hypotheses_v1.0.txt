Candidate Documentary Finding 000017

Title: The Principle of Preserving Multiple Documentary Hypotheses

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes between interpretive possibilities and established
documentary conclusions. Multiple hypotheses may remain compatible with
the evidence until additional criteria justify preferring one.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Enumerate interpretations compatible with the explicit text.
2.  Distinguish possibility from proof.
3.  Preserve competing hypotheses while evidence remains insufficient.
4.  Apply further historical and textual criteria before eliminating
    alternatives.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
