Candidate Documentary Finding 000014

Title: The Principle of Documentary Necessity

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss presents interpretive methods as responses to documentary
necessity rather than independently chosen analytical techniques.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Examine the explicit documentary evidence.
2.  Determine whether an ordinary reading sufficiently explains it.
3.  Introduce additional interpretive operations only when required by
    unresolved documentary problems.
4.  Limit those operations to what the evidence warrants.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
