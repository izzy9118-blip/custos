# Custos

## A living documentary inquiry into Leo Strauss’s practice of reading

> **We examine the text together; GitHub remembers the inquiry.**

Custos reconstructs Leo Strauss’s mode of inquiry as accurately as the documentary evidence permits. It joins shared textual examination to permanent documentary preservation. GitHub records sources, witnesses, provenance, versions, disagreements, decisions, and limits so another investigator can reproduce the inquiry.

> **The corpus governs the system; the system exists to serve the inquiry.**

## Citation Objects

The ordinary unit is one complete documentary problem established by Strauss—normally one governing passage together with one complete note. Each Citation Object proceeds through:

1. first encounter with Strauss;
2. documentary recovery;
3. independent reconstruction of each cited author;
4. comparative return to Strauss; and
5. preservation of findings, alternatives, disagreement, and uncertainty.

Primary texts govern secondary sources. Documentary evidence precedes conclusions. Earlier records are never silently rewritten; corrections proceed forward through linked versions.

## The always-open Inner Sanctum

The ten-phase, thirty-seven-step [Inquiry Architecture Record](records/inquiry-architecture/IAR-000000001.yaml) orders documentary intake, horizon audit, independent reconstruction, authorial authorization, purpose and audience analysis, architectural mapping, problem formation, adversarial testing, progressive disclosure, and synthesis.

The [Inner Sanctum](registers/hermeneutic-object-register/objects/HOC-000000001.yaml) is a **permanent feature of text analysis**. It opens when a canonically bound successor inquiry initializes and remains open through every phase. The complete governed Taxonomy is available from first encounter through final limitation.

This establishes a standing field of attention to placement, sequence, contradiction, implication, repetition, variation, addition, omission, quotation, beginnings, endings, transition, ambiguity, audience, frequency, rarity, prominence, and indirection.

> **The Inner Sanctum is always open; evidence alone activates its techniques.**

Availability never proves presence. A named `LC-###` component becomes eligible for bounded investigation only through its own trigger features, corroboration, ordinary alternatives, disqualifying conditions, source integrity, prohibited inferences, and uncertainty. Activation never proves concealment, hidden teaching, authorial intention, intended audience, or doctrinal truth.

`MAN-000000001` remains the reproducible historical gated release. [MAN-000000002](manifests/cognitive-memory/MAN-000000002.json) is the forward always-open release. This preserves documentary history rather than retroactively changing old runs.
