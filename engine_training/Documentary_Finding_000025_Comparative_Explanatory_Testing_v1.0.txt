Title: The Principle of Comparative Explanatory Testing

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss compares competing explanations against the same documentary
evidence and evaluates them according to their relative explanatory
adequacy.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify competing explanations.
2.  Determine what each explains.
3.  Identify what each leaves unexplained.
4.  Prefer the explanation that best accounts for the total documentary
    record.
