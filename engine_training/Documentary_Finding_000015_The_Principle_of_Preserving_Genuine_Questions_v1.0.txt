Candidate Documentary Finding 000015

Title: The Principle of Preserving Genuine Questions

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes between an inadequate hypothesis and the
documentary problem that gave rise to it. An unsuccessful interpretation
does not invalidate the underlying question if the textual evidence
continues to present a genuine difficulty.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the documentary difficulty prompting an interpretation.
2.  Evaluate the proposed solution independently of the existence of the
    problem.
3.  Reject unsupported explanations where necessary.
4.  Preserve the original question whenever the documentary evidence
    still requires explanation.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
