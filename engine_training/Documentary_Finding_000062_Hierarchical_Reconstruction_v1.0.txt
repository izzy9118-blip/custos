CANDIDATE DOCUMENTARY FINDING 000062

Title: The Principle of Hierarchical Reconstruction

Repository Classification: Documentary Finding
Status: Provisional Documentary Finding
Source Work: Leo Strauss, Persecution and the Art of Writing
Source Essay: “The Literary Character of the Guide for the Perplexed”
Source Context: Strauss’s reconstruction of the relative dignity of
knowledge, demonstration, fiqh, the Guide, and the Mishneh Torah

DOCUMENTARY OBSERVATION

Strauss does not interpret Maimonides’ works or subjects as though
they occupied a flat field of equal importance. He reconstructs the
author’s ordering of activities, kinds of knowledge, subjects, and
writings according to their relative dignity, end, and function.

The hierarchy is recovered from several forms of documentary evidence:
explicit statements, distinctions among kinds of knowledge, the order
of presentation, the placement of subjects within the work, and the
relation between means and ends.

RECOVERED INQUIRY OPERATION

1. Identify the works, subjects, activities, or kinds of knowledge
   whose relative standing is at issue.

2. Recover the author’s explicit distinctions concerning dignity,
   priority, purpose, means, and end.

3. Examine whether the architecture and sequence of the work embody
   or qualify the stated ordering.

4. Distinguish apparent hierarchy from actual hierarchy by testing
   titles, length, historical influence, popularity, and placement
   against the author’s own criteria.

5. Reconstruct the ordering from lower to higher, subordinate to
   governing, or means to end.

6. Interpret each component according to its position and function
   within the recovered hierarchy.

REPOSITORY SIGNIFICANCE

An author’s corpus must not be reconstructed as a mere collection of
equivalent statements or works. Interpretation requires determining
whether the author orders subjects and writings by rank, purpose,
dependence, or dignity.

Hierarchical reconstruction is therefore prior to comparison between
components: the Repository must first determine where each component
stands within the author’s own ordering.

EVIDENTIARY STATUS

Provisional Documentary Finding with elevated evidentiary weight.

The finding arises within Strauss’s sustained methodological study of
literary concealment and is supported by his explicit reconstruction
of competing hierarchies in Maimonides. It remains subject to
refinement or qualification through later corpus-wide comparison.
