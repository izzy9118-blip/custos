Candidate Documentary Finding 000060

Title: The Principle of Progressive Documentary Disclosure

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Source

Leo Strauss, “The Literary Character of the Guide for the Perplexed.”

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs an author’s teaching by following its progression
from early indications and hints to later, more explicit formulation.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1. Identify the earliest indications of the teaching.
2. Trace their development through the sequence of the work.
3. Locate the later explicit formulation.
4. Treat that formulation as the culmination of the preceding
   documentary progression rather than as an isolated statement.
5. Reconstruct the teaching from the complete sequence of disclosure.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding with elevated evidentiary authority
because it appears in Strauss’s sustained methodological investigation
of esoteric writing. It remains open to refinement or qualification by
later documentary evidence.
