Candidate Documentary Finding 000024

Title: The Principle of Explanatory Restraint

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss refrains from adopting the first available explanation for
documentary anomalies. Competing explanations remain open until tested
against the documentary evidence.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the documentary anomaly.
2.  Enumerate the principal explanatory hypotheses.
3.  Evaluate each hypothesis against the documentary evidence.
4.  Adopt an explanation only after comparative testing.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
