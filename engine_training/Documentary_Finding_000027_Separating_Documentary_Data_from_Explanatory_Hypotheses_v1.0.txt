Candidate Documentary Finding 000027

Title: The Principle of Separating Documentary Data from Explanatory
Hypotheses

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss distinguishes between the literary phenomena that require
explanation and the hypotheses proposed to explain them. Documentary
observations are preserved independently of explanatory commitments.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify documentary phenomena.
2.  Preserve observations independently of interpretation.
3.  Formulate competing explanatory hypotheses.
4.  Evaluate hypotheses against the documentary record.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
