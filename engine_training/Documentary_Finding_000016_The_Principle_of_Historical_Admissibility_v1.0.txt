Candidate Documentary Finding 000016

Title: The Principle of Historical Admissibility

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss introduces objective historical criteria that determine whether
an interpretive method is admissible. Esoteric interpretation is
conditioned by demonstrable historical circumstances.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the interpretive method.
2.  Determine its historical prerequisites.
3.  Test those prerequisites against the documentary record.
4.  Apply the method only if the prerequisites are satisfied.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
