Title: The Principle of Compositional Evidence

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss treats compositional design—including style, emphasis,
sequencing, and quotation architecture—as documentary evidence relevant
to interpretation.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Analyze the explicit arguments.
2.  Reconstruct the compositional design.
3.  Identify meaningful patterns of style, placement, and quotation.
4.  Use compositional features as corroborating evidence only when they
    form a coherent pattern.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring corroboration elsewhere in the
corpus.
