Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000100

The Principle of Documentary Structural Mediation

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs authorial intention through the mediating role of
literary structure rather than by relying exclusively upon either
explicit propositions or inferred intentions.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover the explicit propositions of the work.
2.  Reconstruct the literary organization connecting those propositions.
3.  Infer authorial intention from the interaction of propositions and
    structure.
4.  Test the reconstruction against the documentary evidence of the work
    as a whole.

Provisional Documentary Finding.

Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000101

The Principle of Documentary Architectural Coherence

Documentary Observation

Strauss evaluates proposed interpretations according to their coherence
with the reconstructed architecture of the work rather than by isolated
textual plausibility alone.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Form a provisional reconstruction.
2.  Compare it with every relevant documentary feature.
3.  Revise where inconsistencies appear.
4.  Accept only interpretations compatible with the documentary
    architecture as a whole.

Provisional Documentary Finding.

Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000102

The Principle of Documentary Generic Classification

Documentary Observation

Before determining the specific literary character of a work, Strauss
first determines the broader literary genus to which it belongs.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the documentary object.
2.  Determine its broader literary genus.
3.  Determine its distinguishing specific character.
4.  Interpret the work within that generic framework.

Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000103

The Principle of Perspective-Preserving Reconstruction

Documentary Observation

Strauss preserves the author’s own conceptual perspective when
classifying documentary objects rather than silently replacing it with
the investigator’s framework.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover the author’s conceptual categories.
2.  Classify documentary objects using those categories.
3.  Distinguish authorial from investigator classifications.
4.  Preserve that distinction.

The Principle of Teleological Literary Unity

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs individual literary devices as subordinate to a
single governing literary purpose.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover the governing literary intention.
2.  Identify the literary devices.
3.  Explain each through the governing intention.
4.  Test for teleological unity.

Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000105

The Principle of Pedagogically Ordered Concealment

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs literary concealment as ordered toward the author’s
pedagogical purpose rather than as an independent objective.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover the literary purpose.
2.  Determine how concealment contributes.
3.  Interpret concealment as educational unless evidence indicates
    otherwise.
4.  Evaluate techniques by pedagogical contribution.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000108

The Principle of Documentary Relative Emphasis

Documentary Observation

Strauss treats the degree of documentary emphasis devoted to a subject
as evidence of its relative importance within the author’s literary
design.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover discussion of multiple devices.
2.  Compare extent of treatment.
3.  Infer relative importance cautiously.
4.  Test against the remainder of the work.

Candidate Documentary Finding DF-000109

The Principle of Hypothesis-Driven Documentary Inquiry

Documentary Observation

Strauss transforms provisional documentary inferences into explicit
research questions rather than treating them as established conclusions.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Recover documentary evidence.
2.  Formulate a provisional inference.
3.  Express it as a research question.
4.  Continue inquiry before certification.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding.
