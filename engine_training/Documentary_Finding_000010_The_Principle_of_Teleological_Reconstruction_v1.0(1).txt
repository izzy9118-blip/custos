DOCUMENTARY FINDING 000010

Title: The Principle of Teleological Reconstruction

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Before explaining a philosophical practice, Strauss reconstructs the
purpose that the practice is intended to serve.

Means are interpreted through ends rather than ends inferred solely from
observable techniques.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the principal activity.
2.  Determine its governing end.
3.  Reconstruct the obstacles affecting that end.
4.  Explain observed practices as rational means directed toward the
    reconstructed end.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding requiring further corroboration across
the corpus.
