DOCUMENTARY FINDING 000011

Title: The Principle of Reconstructing Conditions of Reception

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss reconstructs not only an author’s intention but also the
conditions under which a teaching could be successfully received by its
intended audience.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Identify the teaching.
2.  Reconstruct the intended audience and prevailing opinions.
3.  Determine intelligible and politically possible forms of
    communication.
4.  Explain literary and pedagogical choices through those reconstructed
    conditions.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional Documentary Finding pending confirmation in later
discussions of esoteric writing and additional corpus evidence.
