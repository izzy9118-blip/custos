Title: The Principle of Internal Documentary Criteria

Status: Provisional Documentary Finding

Documentary Observation

Strauss evaluates an author’s works primarily by criteria recoverable
from the author’s own documentary framework.

Recovered Inquiry Operation

1.  Distinguish internal from external criteria.
2.  Recover the author’s standards.
3.  Apply those standards consistently.
4.  Treat external reception as contextual rather than governing
    evidence.

Evidentiary Status

Provisional pending corroboration or qualification elsewhere in
Strauss’s corpus.
