## I. DECISION

CAG-000000005 establishes CAG-000000006 as the Repository Admission Authority.
The office is active on the effective date only within the final ordinary
native-admission decision jurisdiction defined by AMD-000000002.

CAG-000000002 allocates the CAG-000000006 and DDR-000000001 identifiers. That
allocation is an identifier-governance act only and does not confer, exercise,
or share admission jurisdiction.

## II. RESPONSIBILITIES

CAG-000000006 shall have exactly the following responsibilities:

1. conduct final admission review under Document 000005 as amended;
2. verify the completeness and resolution of required specialized-authority determinations;
3. issue one authorized admission outcome;
4. require coherent object, register, ledger, lifecycle, version, and audit recording;
5. preserve reasons for rejection or return for correction;
6. preserve historical objects and decisions during supersession;
7. refuse admission when authority, evidence, identity, or referential integrity is unresolved.

## III. LIMITATIONS

CAG-000000006 is expressly prohibited from:

1. creating or redefining ontology classes;
2. creating identifier families or allocating identifiers;
3. defining predicates;
4. approving controlled vocabulary;
5. amending constitutional specifications;
6. performing substantive documentary validation for another authorized role;
7. certifying cognitive components;
8. issuing cognitive-integration decisions;
9. releasing Cognitive Memory Manifests;
10. activating production engine components;
11. silently correcting candidate objects or historical records;
12. retroactively curing earlier procedural defects.

## IV. SEPARATION OF POWERS

1. CAG-000000005 establishes the office but does not thereby acquire ordinary
   native-admission jurisdiction.
2. CAG-000000002 allocates identifiers but does not make admission decisions.
3. CAG-000000001, CAG-000000003, and CAG-000000004 retain their specialized
   ontology, predicate, and vocabulary jurisdictions.
4. Specialized determinations remain required inputs where applicable and do
   not become the final admission decision.
5. CAG-000000006 may not assume another authority's jurisdiction to repair a
   missing determination.

## V. COHERENT RECORDING

This establishment action is recorded as one repository unit containing:

1. this establishment instrument and DDR-000000001 decision record;
2. the CAG-000000006 authority record;
3. CAG-000000002 identifier-assignment records;
4. Authority Register version 1.2;
5. Identifier Assignment Ledger version 1.6;
6. corresponding Version Records and integration tests.

## VI. NON-AUTHORIZATIONS

This decision:

1. does not admit Document 000010;
2. does not create the Hermeneutic Object Register;
3. does not admit Strauss's Taxonomy of Literary Concealment;
4. does not allocate HOC or other downstream identifiers;
5. does not validate or certify a cognitive component;
6. does not issue a cognitive-integration decision;
7. does not release a Cognitive Memory Manifest;
8. does not authorize production engine use or Neo4j projection;
9. does not retroactively cure WK-000000001 or any earlier procedural defect.

## VII. NEXT GOVERNED ACTION

production unit is constitutional admission review of Document 000010 under
