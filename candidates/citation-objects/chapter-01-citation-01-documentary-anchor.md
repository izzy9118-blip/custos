## Exact Strauss anchor

> “Above all, Machiavelli says explicitly that in the Prince he will deal solely with principalities and will not discuss republics there since he has done so elsewhere at length.”

The note marker `1` follows “length.”

Immediate documentary context:

- Before the anchor, Strauss states that Machiavelli divides states into republics and principalities and that the title, Epistle Dedicatory, and chapter headings make *The Prince* appear devoted to principalities.
- After the anchor, Strauss says the reference to a work on republics fits the *Discourses* and no other extant or known Machiavellian work, completed or fragmentary.
