I. AUTHORITY AND DEMONSTRATED NEED

The repository owner identified a foundational defect on 2026-07-23:

    "we have a foundational problim. the inner gate should always open

    it must be a main feature of text analysis. compiled and documented"

The active architecture treated Strauss's Taxonomy of Literary Concealment as
a late-stage optional instrument. IAR-000000001 version 1.0 denied access in
phases 1 through 7, the runtime withheld Taxonomy components until phase 8, and
the installed Project instructions described the Inner Sanctum as closed by
default.

That design separated literary perception from ordinary textual analysis. It
allowed the engine to complete documentary intake, horizon audit, independent
reconstruction, authorial authorization, purpose and audience analysis, and
architectural mapping without the distinctions Strauss identifies for
placement, contradiction, omission, repetition, beginnings, transition,
frequency, ambiguity, and indirection being present as standing analytical
possibilities.

This amendment is a substantive forward correction under Custos Codex Rules
XII and XIII. All predecessor files remain preserved as the historical
architecture that revealed the defect.

======================================================================

II. CONSTITUTIONAL DISTINCTION

The Inner Sanctum shall always open at the beginning of text analysis.

"Always open" means:

1. the admitted and manifest-pinned Taxonomy is available as a standing
   perceptual constitution from the first phase;
2. the reasoner may notice, record, compare, and test documentary features in
   light of its distinctions throughout the inquiry;
3. every phase receives the same governed Taxonomy definitions and epistemic
   limits; and
4. the analysis cannot lawfully omit literary form merely because a procedural
   threshold has not yet been reached.

"Always open" does not mean:

1. that concealment is presumed;
2. that every text contains a hidden teaching;
3. that a named technique is present merely because it is available;
4. that oddity, rarity, contradiction, omission, or placement proves intention;
5. that ordinary historical, textual, rhetorical, editorial, or witness
   explanations may be skipped; or
6. that a Taxonomy component may confer documentary, philosophical, or
   certification authority.

Availability is constitutional. Activation is evidentiary.

======================================================================

III. THE LAW OF CONTINUOUS HERMENEUTIC AVAILABILITY

At engine initialization, Custos shall bind the canonical inquiry procedure,
the canonical Hermeneutic Object, and the released Cognitive Memory Manifest.
Once that binding is valid, the Inner Sanctum is open for the complete inquiry.

The full manifest-pinned Taxonomy shall accompany every phase-reasoning
request. It shall not be withheld until adversarial testing, progressive
disclosure, or synthesis.

A particular LC-### component becomes active only when the fixed documentary
record satisfies that component's own requirements, including as applicable:

- minimum trigger features;
- required corroboration;
- ordinary alternatives;
- disqualifying conditions;
- source integrity and attribution;
- independent reconstruction;
- whole-work or corpus testing;
- prohibited inferences; and
- explicit uncertainty.

Failure to activate a component is an analytical result, not closure of the
Inner Sanctum. The component remains available for later evidence without
being treated as present.

======================================================================

IV. RELATION OF OUTER PROCESS AND INNER SANCTUM

The ten-phase, thirty-seven-step inquiry sequence remains mandatory. Its
function is to order evidence, reconstruction, comparison, adversarial
testing, and limitation. It no longer stands outside the Inner Sanctum or
opens it at phase 8.

The relationship is concurrent:

- the inquiry procedure governs what documentary work must be done and in what
  order;
- the Inner Sanctum supplies standing literary distinctions throughout that
  work;
- the evidence determines whether any named distinction is activated; and
- the epistemic hierarchy determines what may be concluded.

The former "Outer Process gate" is therefore prospectively replaced by an
initialization and provenance check. Invalid repository, procedure, Taxonomy,
or Manifest bindings may stop the run. Incomplete phases, unresolved
difficulty, or absent activation evidence may limit conclusions but shall not
close the Inner Sanctum.

======================================================================

V. TEXT-ANALYSIS DUTY

Every substantive textual examination shall attend, where the words permit,
to:

- opening and ending;
- sequence and transition;
- placement and distance;
- repetition and variation;
- addition and omission;
- quotation and misquotation;
- contradiction and implication;
- ambiguity and technical vocabulary;
- speaker, audience, and level of address;
- frequency and rarity;
- architectural prominence and incidentality; and
- the strongest ordinary explanation of each observed feature.

These are not a checklist requiring a positive finding under every heading.
They are a standing field of perception. Custos shall report only features
supported by the fixed text and shall distinguish observation, inference,
hypothesis, question, and uncertainty.

======================================================================

VI. SAFEGUARDS PRESERVED

This amendment preserves and strengthens the following prohibitions:

- no automatic secret-hunting or generalized suspicion;
- no inference of hidden teaching from a trigger alone;
- no selection of a supposedly private teaching without separate evidence;
- no substitution of semantic similarity, graph proximity, or model
  confidence for documentary evidence;
- no transfer across authors, works, audiences, or historical circumstances
  without independent reconstruction;
- no silent repair of missing sources or Documentary Findings;
- no collapse of quotation, observation, interpretation, and user judgment;
- no admission or certification by the engine or model; and
- no conclusion above the evidence.

The safeguard is no longer achieved by blinding early phases to the Taxonomy.
It is achieved by permanent availability joined to component-specific
evidentiary discipline.

The owner's correction is a constitutional design judgment about how Custos
shall analyze texts. It is not represented as a newly recovered proposition
from Strauss or as a Documentary Finding.

    The Inner Sanctum is always open; evidence alone activates its techniques.
